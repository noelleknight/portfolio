My Work

sequence

Sequence is an application for yogis. It's a tool for teachers and practitioners to create full length yoga sequences. The app generates a series of yoga poses based on preferences set by the user. Users are able to save their sequences while logged in so they can use them repeatedly. This was my final project at the Iron Yard and it's one I'm always improving.
